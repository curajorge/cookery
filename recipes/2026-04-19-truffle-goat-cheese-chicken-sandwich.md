# Truffle Goat Cheese & Spiced Chicken Sandwich

**Date:** 2026-04-19
**Serves:** 2 (Dinner) + Leftover marinated chicken
**Prep time:** 15 min | **Cook time:** 12 min | **Total:** 27 min
**Status:** Draft

---

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Chicken Breast (Thin Sliced) | 500g (1.10 lbs) | Pat dry; full amount to be marinated |
| New Zealand Cheddar (Trader Joe's) | 60g | Sliced for 2 sandwiches |
| Marinated Goat Cheese in Truffle | 40g | Use crumbles + some oil for bread |
| Cucumber | 100g | Thinly sliced rounds |
| Baby Arugula | 30g | Tossed in lemon |
| Lemon | 1 unit | Zest for all; juice for "now" portion |
| Grey Poupon Mustard | 15g | For the base spread |
| Butter (Unsalted) | 20g | For searing/bread toasting |
| Olive Oil | 20g | For the marinade |
| **Spices** (Salt, Pepper, Garlic/Onion Powder, Oregano) | 10g total | **No paprika**; see method |
| Sourdough or Baguette | 2 portions | High quality |

## Method

1. **The Base Marinade (Full 1.10 lbs):** In a bowl, combine the chicken with 5g (approx 1 tsp) kosher salt, 2g black pepper, 1g garlic powder, 1g onion powder, and 1g dried oregano. Add the olive oil and the **zest of the entire lemon**. Toss to coat thoroughly. 
   - **Overnight Batch:** Separate the chicken you aren't eating tonight into a container. *Science: The zest provides essential oils for flavor without the acid that breaks down protein fibers over long periods.*
   - **Now Batch:** Add a squeeze of lemon juice to the pieces for tonight and let sit for 5-10 mins.

2. **Cucumber & Arugula:** Slice cucumbers thin. Toss with a pinch of salt. Toss arugula with lemon juice and zest.

3. **The Sear:** Heat the smart oven pan or a skillet with butter and a teaspoon of the truffle oil from the goat cheese. Sear chicken at high heat (400°F/200°C) for 3-4 minutes per side. Pull when the internal temperature hits **150°F (65°C)**.

4. **The Melt:** Top with the New Zealand Cheddar during the last 60 seconds of cooking.

5. **Assembly:** 
   - Spread Grey Poupon on toasted bread.
   - Layer: Spiced chicken/cheddar, truffle goat cheese crumbles, cucumber, and lemon-arugula.

## Quick Rationale

By using lemon zest and dried oregano instead of smoked paprika, we lean into a Mediterranean flavor profile that highlights the truffle and sharp cheddar. Avoiding lemon juice in the overnight batch prevents the thin-sliced chicken from becoming mushy or "chalky" due to acid-driven denaturing.

## Notes & Adjustments

- **Overnight chicken:** Before cooking tomorrow, give it a fresh squeeze of lemon juice 5 minutes before it hits the pan to brighten the flavors.
- **Spice Swap:** The onion powder and oregano provide a savory "herbes de Provence" vibe without the smoky intensity you dislike.
