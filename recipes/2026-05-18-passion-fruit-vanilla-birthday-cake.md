# Passion Fruit & Vanilla Birthday Cake

**Serves:** 5
**Prep time:** 45 min | **Cook time:** 30 min | **Total:** 75 min

## Method

**White Chocolate Mascarpone Cream (Make First)**
1. Melt the white chocolate over a double boiler until smooth (about 110°F/43°C). Let it cool slightly to 85°F/29°C.
2. In a chilled bowl, whip the cold mascarpone (150g) and heavy cream (100g) together until soft peaks form. *Science note: Cold fat is essential for emulsion stability and trapping air.*
3. Quickly fold the cooled melted white chocolate and salt (1g) into the whipped cream mixture until stiff and pipeable. Chill until ready to use.

**Vanilla Cake (Reverse Creaming Method)**
4. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line two 6-inch cake pans with parchment paper.
5. In a stand mixer, combine cake flour (150g), sugar (150g), baking powder (6g), and salt (3g). Add room temperature butter (85g) and mix on low speed until it resembles coarse sand. *Science note: The reverse creaming method coats flour proteins in fat before hydration, minimizing gluten development for a fine, velvety crumb.*
6. Whisk together the eggs (100g), milk (90g), and vanilla (8g). Add half of this liquid to the dry ingredients and beat on medium speed for 1 minute to build structure.
7. Add the remaining liquid and beat for another 30 seconds until just incorporated.
8. Divide batter evenly between the two pans. Bake for 25-30 minutes until the internal temperature reaches 200°F (93°C) and a toothpick comes out clean.
9. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.

**Assembly**
10. Level the two cooled cakes by trimming the rounded tops. Cut one of the cakes in half horizontally, giving you three thin cake layers (you will have one extra half-layer left over).
11. **Filling 1 prep:** Gently fold the Coconut Cult Passion Fruit Yogurt (120g) with cold mascarpone (50g) until combined. This stabilizes the funky, acidic yogurt and prevents it from making the cake soggy.
12. **Layer 1:** Place the first cake layer down. Spread the stabilized Passion Fruit Yogurt filling evenly.
13. **Layer 2:** Top with the second cake layer. Spread a thick layer of the White Chocolate Mascarpone Cream (Filling 2). The sweetness and fat of this cream will directly balance the intense acidity and funk of the probiotic yogurt.
14. **Layer 3:** Top with the third cake layer. Use the remaining White Chocolate Mascarpone Cream to frost the top and outside of the cake in a thin, rustic "naked" style so it's not too heavy.
15. Chill for at least 1 hour before slicing to allow the fillings to set.

## Quick Rationale

The intense, highly-fermented profile of the Coconut Cult yogurt requires a high-fat, high-sweetness counterweight; the white chocolate mascarpone cream acts as this buffer, bridging the sharp acidity with mellow dairy richness. The reverse creaming cake method guarantees a tight but tender crumb that won't disintegrate under the moisture of the yogurt. Stabilizing the yogurt with a small amount of mascarpone prevents syneresis (weeping) into the cake layers.

## Notes & Adjustments

- Ensure all cake ingredients are at room temperature to maintain the batter emulsion.
- You can build the cake as a 4-layer cake by cutting both 6-inch cakes in half, alternating the yogurt and white chocolate fillings.
