Valentine's Dinner for Two

Mise en Place & Action Plan — 2026-02-14

Salt the Steak Do First

  1. Pat the 620g strip bone dry with paper towels
  2. Weigh out 6g kosher salt (~1.5 tsp Diamond Crystal)
  3. Season all sides evenly
  4. Place on a wire rack, leave uncovered — sits 45 min minimum while you work

Make Pasta Dough Do First

IngredientAmount
00 flour200g
Fine salt2g
Whole eggs2 (~100g)
Egg yolk1 (~17g)
  1. Flour + salt in bowl, paddle attachment
  2. Add eggs + yolk, mix low until shaggy — 1–2 min
  3. Switch to dough hook, medium-low 8–10 min until smooth & elastic
  4. Wrap tight in plastic, rest 30 min minimum

Roll & Cut Pasta

After 30 min dough rest

  1. Flour the surface generously
  2. Roll to 2mm thick — rotate, don't stretch
  3. Lightly flour, fold loosely, cut 2–3mm strips (tonnarelli)
  4. Toss in semolina (30g), nest loosely on a tray

Steak Into the Oven

1 Oven Pro → Bake → 200°F, wire rack over tray
2 Probe thermometer in the thickest part
3 Walk away — 45–55 min to 120°F
Everything is prepped. You're just waiting on the probe now.
